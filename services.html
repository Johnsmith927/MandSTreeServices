Our Services

Professional tree care for Pittsburgh and surrounding areas

Tree Removal

We safely remove trees of any size - from small backyard trees to massive 80-foot specimens. Whether it's near your house, over power lines, or in a tight backyard, we have the equipment and expertise to do it safely.

What We Remove:

  • Trees near homes and structures - We use ropes and rigging to carefully lower sections without damage
  • Trees around power lines - Safe clearance working with utility companies when needed
  • Storm-damaged trees - Fast response for fallen or leaning trees after storms
  • Dead or dying trees - Remove before they become a hazard
  • Multiple trees - Clearing land for construction or landscaping projects

Our Process: We assess each job, determine the safest removal method, protect your property, remove the tree in sections if needed, and haul away all debris. Your yard will be cleaner than we found it.

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Expert tree trimming to keep your property safe and your trees healthy. Our skilled climbers work safely at any height to carefully remove problem branches around roofs, power lines, and other structures.

What We Trim:

  • Branches over roofs - Prevent damage and remove debris-dropping limbs
  • Power line clearance - Safe trimming around electrical lines
  • Overgrown trees - Crown reduction to restore proper size and shape
  • Dead branches - Remove before they fall and cause damage
  • View restoration - Strategic trimming to open up views without harming the tree

Professional Technique: We make proper cuts that promote healing and don't leave ugly stubs. All small branches are chipped and hauled away, larger sections can be left for firewood if you want.

Stump Grinding

Complete stump removal using professional grinding equipment. We grind stumps below ground level so you can plant grass, landscape, or build over the area. All wood chips are cleaned up or can be left as free mulch.

Benefits of Stump Removal:

  • No more tripping hazards - Safe for kids, pets, and lawn mowers
  • Prevents pest problems - Stops termites, carpenter ants, and beetles
  • Opens up space - Use the area for landscaping, patio, or grass
  • No new sprouts - Grinding prevents the tree from growing back
  • Improves appearance - Clean, level yard looks much better

Pricing: Based on stump diameter and accessibility. We provide free estimates and can typically grind stumps the same day we remove a tree.
